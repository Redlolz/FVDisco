]]> Piston Powered Mechanical Redstone Calculator

The Calculatron 3000!

  • Addition
  • Subtraction
  • Compact design

This relatively compact design uses signal length encoding to turn bi-directional piston loop decoders to keep track of the total.

Can add or subtract any two digit numbers up to a total of 999. As it uses no binary, its more akin to a mechanical device with gears and cogs allowing for a compact design. Could in theory be modified for multiplication and division, but i only had a few days to design it.

Notes:

  • Input 2 digit numbers e.g 02, 99
  • Wait for the numbers to spin up before entering another digit
