Behind the Temple of Notch: A look behind the scenes at The Temple of Notch

Updated to work in Minecraft 1.4.x

Notches judgment is randomly calculated based on how long it takes the player to reach the temple after placing the offering in the well. The animations are controlled by pistons and repeater delays.

Contrary to popular belief, what or where you place in the well has no bearing on the outcome ;)
