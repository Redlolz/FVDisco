Plants vs Zombies: Playable Zombie game.

Defend your home from the Zompocalypse! A Minecraft version of the popular game Plants vs Zombies.

Features:

  • Auto firing flowers
  • Land mines
  • House damage detection
  • Gold rewards and Purchase system
  • Huge Sunflower!!

Instructions:

  • Play in Easy mode
  • Purchase flowers with gold by dropping gold in each feeding well (this will activate the flower for 5 mins)
  • Game is over when your house health reaches zero

Click the downloadnow! button to try it out for yourself.
