Programmable Drum Kit
Posted: December 12, 2011 at 4:32 pm

Programmable Drum Kit: Animated drums, programmable in real time.

Features:

  • No mods needed
  • 3 channels @ 16 steps each
  • Animated drums

Quick and easy to use, lay down some beats and watch the drums beat in perfect sync to the Noteblocks.

Instructions:

  • Place wool on the tracks to define the rhythm.
  • Green button starts/stops the machine.
  • Other buttons mute individual drums.
