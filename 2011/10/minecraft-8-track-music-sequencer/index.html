8 Track Music Sequencer
Posted: October 27, 2011 at 12:52 pm

8 Track Music Sequencer: 8 Channel Noteblock sequencer, never have to move along with note blocks again!

Updated to work in Minecraft 1.4.x

Features:

  • Stores up to 1024 notes
  • 8 Controllable Channels
  • DNA Inspired design
  • Expandable

Programmed with my own arrangement of J. Pachelbel’s Canon in D (transposed to F#)

This was featured and remixed by C418 in his album “one” listen here .   Also featured in the Minecraft documentary “The Story of Mojang”.
