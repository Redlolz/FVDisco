Cake Defense
Posted: October 13, 2011 at 6:36 pm

Cake Defense: Defend your cake from Herobrine’s Minions over 12 levels of increasing difficulty.

Updated to work in Minecraft 1.4.x

Features:

  • Full color screen
  • Random armor, weapons and food drops at the end of each wave
  • 12 levels of increasing difficulty
  • Massive reward at the end of level 12
  • Cake!

Press the “Next wave” button to begin, the screen will display the mob types, multiplier and wave number. After the wave you will be rewarded random armor, weapons and food drops. There is a chest next to the button for basic weapons and supplies.

Click the downloadnow! button to try it out for yourself.
