FVDisco: Frequently Asked Questions.

 

Do you work alone or with a team?

For 99% of my builds I work alone.  I collaborated with Sethbling on Super Pirate Battle Royal.  For the upcoming map, The Labyrinth, I am working with CNBMinecraft and a small team.

 

When is the Labyrinth being released?

The Labyrinth is one of the most ambitious projects ever attempted in Minecraft.  It will be released when it is ready ;)

 

Where are you from?

I grew up in the UK, but currently reside in Australia.

 

Why don’t you talk in your videos?

I like to communicate visually, this breaks any language barriers, giving my videos a universal appeal.  Also, I enjoy the challenge of making my videos informative and entertaining, without commentary and let the build do the talking :D

 

What software do you use to make videos?

I use Fraps to record footage and Adobe Premiere Pro to edit.

 

Do you really have OCD?

No.  Although I do consider myself a perfectionist.
